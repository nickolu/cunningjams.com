Here's how ChatGPT writes now.

Like this.

Short little phrases. Punchy four word sentences. Peppered-in metaphors.

Think it's not obvious?

It's no longer the em-dash that will give you away. It's the fear of rambling.

But honestly what's worse, being obviously written by ChatGPT, or being overly wordy, rambling, and meandering? Personally, I'm afraid my written communication comes off as sophomoric, because I tend to ramble A LOT. I'm not sure. I'm tempted to have ChatGPT critique my writing of this post, but I'll resist this time and see what happens.
