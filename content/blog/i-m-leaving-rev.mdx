As I leave [**Rev Intelligence**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/revintelligence/) to start a new journey in my life,  I want to take a moment to express my gratitude to the many incredible people who have supported me along the way. While this list isn’t exhaustive, these are a few key individuals who have had a particularly profound impact on my journey:

1\. [**Mike Bennett**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mikebennett2/) has been an endless source of support and wisdom. His example and advice have taught me so much about being an effective leader on a software team.

2\. [**Sean Dwyer**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sdwyer/) is the person I point to when I need to prove I'm a "real" engineer. Since I don't have a strong academic background, I owe so much of my technical growth to Sean's incredible mentorship. My code style, knowledge of architecture, UI best practices, and more are all influenced by him in some way.

3\. [**Jonathan Spier**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jspier/) is the kind of CEO everyone dreams of having. Approachable, a great listener, and genuinely invested in his team, he gave me trust, amplified my voice within the company, and provided invaluable mentorship on how to be a strong team leader and manager.
