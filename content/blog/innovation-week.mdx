“Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower.” — Steve Jobs

At [**Rev Intelligence**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/revintelligence/) we just completed our semiannual innovation week, where everyone on the build team gets the chance to create anything they dream up. I was amazed by what my team members built this time around. I believe a significant reason our products and services are so successful for our customers is the incredible internal tools and product innovations we develop during these bursts of creativity.
