This post marks an interesting turn. In the past, I might share the project directly so you can use it too, but now, I think the instructions for the project are possibly more useful.

It's sort of like how replays in smash brothers works. If you're not familiar, Super Smash Bros is a fighting game where you play as various Nintendo characters and try to smash each other off the stage. When saving a replay, instead of storing a large video file, Nintendo had the clever idea to just save the inputs and their timing. That way, when the user runs a replay, instead of watching a video, they're actually just spinning up the game engine and watching a deterministic battle play out.
